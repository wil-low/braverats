## **R (Braverats!)**

* **Game design:** Seiji Kanai
* **Players:** 2
* **Game time:** ~5 minutes

---

## **Story**

After the sudden passing of the benevolent King, the country falls into turmoil. With no direct successor, uncertainty spreads among the people.

Two powerful Count Princes rise as contenders:

* **Blue Prince** — calm, composed, strong-willed.
* **Red Prince** — passionate, intelligent, and bold.

They gather their allies and begin a silent struggle for the crown.

But each harbors a secret:

* The Blue Prince loves the Red Prince’s sister.
* The Red Prince loves the Blue Prince’s sister.

Who will overcome this weakness and claim the throne?

---

# **Game Overview**

Each player represents one Prince and competes for influence.

* Both players simultaneously choose a card each round.
* Each card has:

  * A **strength value**
  * A **mandatory special power**
* The first player to win **4 rounds** becomes the new King.

If both players run out of cards before either reaches 4 points, the game ends in a draw.

---

# **Setup**

Each player receives **8 cards** of one color:

* Jester
* Lady
* Spy
* Assassin
* Counsellor
* Magician
* General
* Prince

The Red and Blue versions are identical in effect.

These 8 cards form the player’s hand.

---

# **Round Structure**

1. Each player selects one card secretly.
2. Cards are revealed simultaneously.
3. Special powers resolve (mandatory).
4. The winner is determined.
5. Used cards remain face up.

   * The losing card is turned sideways for clarity.
6. Repeat until someone wins 4 rounds or cards run out.

---

# **Draws & "On Hold" Rounds**

If both cards result in a draw:

* The round is **put on hold**.
* The winner of the next decisive round:

  * Wins that round
  * Also wins all held rounds

Multiple rounds may accumulate on hold.

---

# **Card List**

---

## **Jester (0)**

**Power:** *This round is nullified and put on hold.*

* Overrides all powers **except Magician**.
* No winner this round.
* Round is placed on hold.

---

## **Lady (1)**

**Power:** *If the opponent played the Prince, you win the game.*

* Immediate game win.
* Ignores number of points.

---

## **Spy (2)**

**Power:** *Next round, your opponent reveals their card before you choose yours.*

* Affects the next round.
* If both players play Spy, effects cancel.

---

## **Assassin (3)**

**Power:** *Lowest strength wins.*

* Strength comparison is inverted.
* Applies after other relevant effects.

---

## **Counsellor (4)**

**Power:** *If you win with this card, it counts as 2 points.*

* Applies even if the round was on hold.
* If previous rounds were on hold:

  * Victory may be worth 3+ points.
* If both players play Counsellor:

  * Effect does not stack.

---

## **Magician (5)**

**Power:** *Nullifies the opponent’s special power.*

* Only strength values are compared.
* Does not affect General’s delayed +2 from a previous round.

---

## **General (6)**

**Power:** *Your card next turn gets +2 strength.*

* Applies to the next round.
* Cannot be nullified by a Magician played in the following round.

---

## **Prince (7)**

**Power:** *You win the round.*

* Wins regardless of strength.
* Assassin does not override this.
* Jester still puts the round on hold.
* If both players play Prince:

  * Powers cancel.
  * Compare strength normally (usually a draw unless modified).

---

# **Game End**

The game ends immediately when:

* A player reaches **4 points**, or
* A Lady defeats a Prince (instant win condition).

If all cards are used and no player has 4 points, the game is a **draw**.
